About EmojiVerse Studio

EmojiVerse Studio is a fully client-side image editor that reimagines your photos using emoji as the smallest unit of colour — like a modern mosaic tile. Every render happens on your device with the Canvas API: nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Built with HTML5, CSS3 and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no backend, no tracking.
